What about long-term NCDs?

F-TRAC covers short-term instruments only — CP, CD, and CB Repo (NCDs <1yr maturity). Long-term NCD primary issuances are mandatorily reported on SEBI's EBP platform and traded on NSE CBRICS and BSE WDM — both require registered participant login and are not publicly scrapable. For NCD secondary market data, consider NSE Bonds ↗ or BSE Debt Trade Report ↗ (require browser login).
